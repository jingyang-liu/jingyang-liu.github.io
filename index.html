Jingyang Liu 刘竞阳

jingyang.liu[at]mail.utoronto.ca

I am a Ph.D. student in The Edward S. Rogers Sr. Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Toronto, working with Prof. Natalie Enright Jerger

Research interests: computer architecture, sustainable computing

Before joining the University of Toronto, I received my M.S. degree in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign, where I worked on the design-for-test (DfT) infrastructue and bootup sequence of a wafer-scale processor, advised by Prof. Rakesh Kumar. I also received my B.S degree in Electrical Engineering from UIUC ECE.
